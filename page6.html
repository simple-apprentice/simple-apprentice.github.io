My
Experience

In April 2020, I represented Nanjing Agricultural University to participate in the Nanjing Science and Technology Exhibition. In the activity, we exchanged our competition experience and techniques with other universities and presented our research results to high school students. We explained the fundamental principle of the robot and invited high school students to operate it themselves. On this basis, we guided several high school students to build a simple robot system, increasing their enthusiasm for scientific research.

Through the exchange, I learned about the sensors and algorithms used by other universities, which significantly enhanced our technologies. Additionally, I learned about competitions in which we had never previously participated. After that, we attempted new challenges, including the Robotics Skills Competition and Drone Competition.
