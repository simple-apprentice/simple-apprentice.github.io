Biped Robot 

The competition simulates a human running scenario, requiring robots to complete a specified distance in a limited time. Furthermore, it needs to complete the required actions on the way, such as flipping and dancing.The project finally won the third prize in Jiangsu Robot Competition.

In terms of hardware, the biped robot used six bus servos to simulate the human body, MPU6050 to correct posture, condenser microphone module to automatically respond to the voice command, which can make required movements.

On the software side, we applied the PID algorithm to the servo to minimize the servo angle error as much as possible. In order to ensure the travel path stays on course, the camera was used to gather information about the road for calculating the distance between the center line and the edge.

(Due to the confidentiality agreement, our code cannot be made public, if you want to know the code, please get in touch with me.)
