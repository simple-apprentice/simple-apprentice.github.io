My
Experience

In May 2019, the secretariat of the AI Development Association, where I was located, was responsible for hosting the Robot Competition. I served as the host and referee assistant.

The specific requirement of the competition is to design the driving route with the most points according to the map and complete the specified actions in the specified location. My responsibility was to monitor the competition and assist the referee in calculating the score, which gives me a unique perspective on the competition. In the process, I gained a deeper understanding of designing and implementing more efficient strategies.

Through the activity, I deeply understood the competition process and met many excellent seniors, which is why I joined the robot laboratory in the future.
